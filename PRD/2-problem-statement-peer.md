## Step 4: If/Then Statement (Actionable Proposal)

**If the app sends scheduled afternoon reminders that prompt Berci to complete a small, clearly defined portion of his assignment (such as one slide or one paragraph), then he will begin working earlier and avoid last-minute preparation before presentations.**

---

## Proposed App Concept (Based on Interview Insights)

A minimalist reminder and assignment management app designed for students that:

* Sends reminders during the user’s most productive time (afternoon).
* Breaks assignments into small, manageable parts.
* Allows users to prioritize the hardest section first.
* Uses a simple, mind-map-style visual layout.
* Requires progress confirmation to encourage accountability.
