# User Persona

**Name:** Emma Carter
**Age/Grade:** 17, 12th Grade
**School Type:** Private, high-achieving

---

## Demographics

* Attends a private high school, focused on college preparation.
* High-achieving academically.
* Aims for Ivy League admission (e.g., Princeton).

---

## Daily Schedule

* **Wake-up:** 6:00 AM
* **School:** 8:00 AM – 3:30 PM
* **Extracurriculars:** Club activities and school volleyball, practice 4:00 – 6:00 PM
* **Study:** 6:30 – 7:30 PM, no breaks, mainly solo studying
* **Dinner:** 7:30 PM
* **Evening free time:** 8:00 – 10:00 PM, social media and relaxation
* **Bedtime:** 9:30 – 10:00 PM

---

## Technical Literacy & Tools

* Devices: iPhone, MacBook, AirPods Max
* Uses Notes app and calendar/alarms to track homework, tests, and deadlines
* Keeps productivity tools minimal but is open to trying new apps
* Color codes notes minimally
* Notifications help occasionally; often keeps DND on

---

## Current Methods for Tracking

* Notes app for assignments and deadlines
* Calendar/alarms for reminders
* Mostly self-managed; minimal collaboration or reliance on peers

---

## Goals

* Gain admission to an Ivy League university
* Maintain GPA of 3.8–4.0
* Achieve personal growth and validation from family and self
* Success is defined by academic achievement, making loved ones proud, and beginning university life

---

## Frustrations & Pain Points

* Feels “cramped” and unsure of what to tackle first
* Overwhelmed after practice, especially during university application season
* Feels unproductive despite putting in high effort
* Forgetting to list assignments in Notes app creates additional stress
* Experiences pressure from multiple important assignments simultaneously

---

## Emotional Landscape

* Feels tiredness and pressure after extracurriculars
* Experiences anxiety and guilt when assignments pile up
* Internal dialogue often includes self-criticism for feeling lazy
* Struggles with organizing tasks when energy is low

---

## Social & Support

* Primarily independent in studying
* Occasionally seeks help from teachers
* Social life mostly on weekends; uses this time to disconnect and recharge

---

## Personality & Behavior

* Highly motivated, achievement-oriented, and disciplined
* Balances high workload with minimal but meaningful social and leisure activities
* Prefers functional, minimalistic organization but appreciates small aesthetic touches (e.g., light color coding)

---

This persona captures Emma as a highly motivated, independent, and organized student who struggles with occasional overwhelm due to high stakes and a packed schedule. Her behavior, frustrations, and goals provide a clear target for designing a to-do and scheduling app tailored to her needs.
